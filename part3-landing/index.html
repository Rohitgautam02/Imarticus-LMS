FAQ

Will I be able to pursue a master's degree later? +

Yes. With a UGC-recognised parallel degree, you can pursue MBA programmes in India and abroad through exams like CAT and GMAT. You can also take certifications like CPA, CFA, ACCA, CMA and FRM. Government jobs through UPSC are also possible.

What career pathways does ISFB prepare me for? +

ISFB prepares you for placements in corporate roles at IB, consulting, fintech, and wealth management. You can also pursue entrepreneurship backed by BlinC's Rs 25Cr Finance Venture Fund or go for Global Masters powered by the Ambitio platform.

Is the BBA degree recognised? +

Yes. The online BBA offered through the recommended colleges like NMIMS and Symbiosis are fully UGC-recognised and accepted by companies, government bodies, and academic institutions globally. It holds equal weight as a traditional degree.

What is the admission process? +

Submit the application form on this page, attend a counselling session, and complete the admission formalities. The Round 3 deadline is 30th April 2026.

Who can apply for this program? +

Students who have completed or are appearing for their Class 12 examinations can apply. The program is open to students from all streams - Commerce, Science, and Arts.

Where is the campus located? +

The ISFB campus is located in Powai, Mumbai - in the heart of India's financial capital, surrounded by global corporates, banks, and fintechs.

Is hostel accommodation available? +

Yes, the program offers an opt-in residence option. Students can choose to stay on campus for a fully immersive learning experience.

Are scholarships available? +

Yes, ISFB offers merit-based and need-based scholarships. Contact the admissions team for detailed information about scholarship eligibility and application process.

What are the fee payment options? +

The program offers flexible payment options including semester-wise payments and EMI facilities through partner banks. Detailed fee structure is shared during the counselling session.
